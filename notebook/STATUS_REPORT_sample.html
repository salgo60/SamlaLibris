External Link Health Study – RAA (Sample)

Last updated: 2025-12-01

This is a pilot measurement based on 300 sampled links from the full dataset.

Scope and method

This page reports HTTP availability of external links used in Wikipedia, with a focus on links to Riksantikvarieämbetet and related domains. It measures technical accessibility of URLs, not the quality of their content.
